Visualizations of Weather and Latitude

The purpose of this project was to compare various weather-related measurements with their north/south position on the earth's surface. These included temperature, cloudiness, relative humidity, and wind speed. The readings for this project were taken in the Fall for the Northern Hemisphere.

As an additional piece of context, the points are also plotted based on their actual position on the globe and not purely latitude. These additional graphics are contained in a bonus page of this homework assignment.
