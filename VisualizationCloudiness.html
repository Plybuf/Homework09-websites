Latitude and Cloudiness

There is only a small correlation between cloudiness and the latitude of the city. It is cloudier in the upper northern latitudes, but this is also the location of most of the results.

Latitude and Cloudiness (Positional)

Viewing the data based on their actual global position paints a clearer picture of the global cloudiness distribution. The world's deserts are somewhat visible along the 20-30 degree latitude lines. This view also highlights that much of the cloudiness in the northern hemisphere is due to a weather system traveling across North America at the time.
