Latitude and Wind Speed

There is a small correlation between wind speed and the latitude of the city, primary in several bands (40 and 65 degrees north and the equator). In the fall it is typically windier at the higher latitudes due to the change in length of sunlight and the resulting pressure system changes.

Latitude and Wind Speed (Positional)

The correlation between windspeeds on the globe shows a bit more context compared to the graph shown above. Coastal areas are have higher wind speeds, primarily due to the constant pressure differences between the water and the land.
