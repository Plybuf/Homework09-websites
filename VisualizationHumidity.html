Latitude and Relative Humidity

There is a correlation between humidity and the latitude of the city. The relative humidity of cities is higher near the equator and in the latitudes between 40 and 60 degrees. Desert latitudes are also visible, with clusters of lower relative humidity between 20 and 30 degrees latitude.

Latitude and Relative Humidity (Positional)

Viewing the data based on their actual global position paints a clearer picture of the global relative humidity distribution. The world's deserts are clearly visible along the 20-30 degree latitude lines in both hemispheres. The Sahara, Arabian and Kalahari Deserts are especially visible.
